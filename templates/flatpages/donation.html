How does it work

Use Feedmee to find food

By using Feedmee app when dining out, you help to donate a meal to someone in need.

Book tables or order deliveries

Every time you book a table or order delivery through the app, the service providers pay us a small fee.

And then you help to give back

We then donate a portion of that revenue to a local food charity, covering the cost of a meal for someone in need.
