Things you can do on Feedmee

Table booking

You can book a table online through 3 simple steps and get instant confirmation. We partner with different booking providers around the world so you can keep collecting the royalty points.

Order food delivery

You can order meals on Feedmee through the services provided by our on-demand delivery partners. Once you have found the food you want, ordering and getting it delivered is just a click away.

Find recipe inspirations

If you don’t feel like eating out, Feedmee also shows you recipes of the dishes that you like so you can cook at home. All recipes are crafted by recipe specialists. It’s all free, too!
